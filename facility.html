EAT & DRINK

Restaurants & In-Room Dining

RESTAURANT : WINGS - TASTE THE WORLD

Our restaurant Wings is inspired from the grey, black, with pale brownish wings of the magnificent bird Jujurana.

It is a multi-cuisine all-day dining restaurant, set up in a 2000 sq ft area which is centrally air-conditioned.

Opening up into a terrace with an unhindered view of the Kanga valley all up to the Pong Dam. A blend of lip-smacking cuisine and a view to die for. Aren’t that what vacations are all about?

Our whole menu is perfect yet we’ll say you should definitely try our Wings specialty : chicken wings in various ways with that drink you need. Wings organizes special live music on weekends to give an experience which is out of this world for our outstation guests and locals of Dharamshala.

CASUAL BAR : HP68

Open from 11am till 11pm. A Casual seating style opening into the terrace overlooking the valley. Offering a variety of cocktails, mocktails and liquor along with a finger snack menu curated for the tipsy . It’s leisure, it’s peculiar; yet, it’s unabashedly Himachali. Hp 68 – offers anything but the usual. The menu is delightful, defies stereotypes, and boldly brings quirky simple dishes and comfort food to the table. All backed by inspired cocktails and a solid bar.

COFFEE & PASTRY SHOP : JACU

For the ones who enjoy a hot or cold brew, with a view… we have; Jacu. A coffee shop serving the best coffees, sandwiches and snacks for you to fill up on before the day starts, or end your day with. Not just that, we also have an elaborate assortment of mouth watering pastries and cakes to accompany your coffee. We have you covered!
